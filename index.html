About Me

Hello, I’m Dorcas Osawaru, a dedicated and versatile Virtual Assistant with a background in computer instruction and data entry. After working in-person for a small business, I transitioned into the virtual space and completed the ALX Africa Virtual Assistant program — equipping me with professional training and hands-on digital experience. I specialize in web support and design, HTML & CSS, social media management, and content creation. My services include everything from landing page setup and video creation in Canva to email and calendar management, survey form creation, and document formatting in Word, Excel, and Google Docs. I’m proficient in tools such as Canva, Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, Zoom, Trello, and more — always quick to learn and adapt to new platforms. Whether I’m planning social media content, managing inboxes, or building simple web pages, I bring resourcefulness, adaptability, attention to detail, and a growth mindset to every project. What drives me? Helping entrepreneurs and small businesses stay organized, show up professionally online, and focus on what they do best — while I handle the rest.
